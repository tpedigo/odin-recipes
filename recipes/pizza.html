Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a pepperoni pizza. It is quick and easy and great for a weekday meal.

Even though it's not the healthiest of meals, a pepperoni pizza is sure to make you happy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza dough
  • 1 can pizza sauce
  • 3 cups mozzerella cheese
  • 20 slices pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough
  2. Spoon sauce on top and spread evenly
  3. Shred cheese and sprinkle evenly over sauce
  4. Lay pepperoni slices on top of cheese
  5. Bake in 375ÂºF for 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown

Enjoy!
