Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for a classic PB&J sandwich. It is quick and easy and a great option to pack for kids' lunches.

You can substitute whatever flavor jelly you like, and use crunchy or smooth peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons jelly
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. Spread jelly on one slice of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on other slice of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together so that the jelly and peanut butter touch

Enjoy!
