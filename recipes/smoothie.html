Smoothie

Description

This is a recipe for a smoothie. It is a great option for a healthy breakfast or dessert.

Have fun with this recipe and add in whatever fruits or other ingredients you want for flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 2 cups milk or liquid of choice
  • Optional: nut butter

Steps

  1. Put banana, berries, and milk in blender
  2. Blend on high until mixture is smooth and no chunks remain
  3. Optional: top with nut butter, granola, coconut, or other toppings

Enjoy!
